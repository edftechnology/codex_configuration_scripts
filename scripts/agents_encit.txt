# AGENTS.md

## Purpose
This document defines the formatting and submission requirements for papers to be included in the Proceedings of ENCIT 2026. All agents and contributors must strictly follow these guidelines.

---

## General Requirements

- Papers must be written in **English**.
- Maximum length: **8 pages**, including figures, tables, and references.
- Format: **A4 size**.
- Font: **Times New Roman, size 10** (except title, abstract, etc.).
- Line spacing: **single**.
- Margins:
  - First page: top = 3 cm; others = 2 cm.
  - Remaining pages: all margins = 2 cm.
- Pages **must NOT be numbered**.
- Text must be **justified**.
- First line of each paragraph must be indented by **0.5 cm**.
- Use **SI units** exclusively.

---

## Title and Author Section

- Title must be in **uppercase**.
- Include:
  - Paper code
  - Full title
  - Authors and affiliations
  - Emails
- Abstract:
  - ~200 words
  - No formulas or references
- Keywords:
  - Up to 5 keywords
  - Separated by commas

---

## Sections and Headings

- Use numbered sections (Arabic numerals).
- Maximum of **3 levels** of subsections.
- Font: Times New Roman, bold, size 10.
- Leave one blank line before and after each heading.

---

## Equations

- Must be:
  - Indented by 0.5 cm
  - Italicized
  - Numbered (right-aligned, in parentheses)
- Referencing:
  - Mid-sentence: `Eq. (1)`
  - Beginning: `Equation (1)`
- Define all symbols near first use.

---

## Figures

- Place close to first reference in text.
- Number sequentially.
- Caption:
  - Max 3 lines
  - Centered
  - Font size 10
- Use formats:
  - GIF (≤16 colors)
  - JPEG (>16 colors)

---

## Tables

- Must be centered.
- Titles:
  - Max 3 lines
  - Similar font to body text
- Use SI units.
- Explanations go **below** the table (not inside).

---

## References

- Must be cited in text using:
  - Author–year format
- Examples:
  - (Author, 2023)
  - Author (2023)
- For 3+ authors: use "et al."
- List at the end:
  - Alphabetical order
  - First line aligned left
  - Remaining lines indented (0.5 cm)

---

## Acknowledgements

- Optional section before references.

---

## Responsibility Notice

Include at the end:

> The author(s) is (are) solely responsible for the printed material included in this paper.

---

## Compliance

All submissions that do not strictly follow these guidelines may be rejected.
